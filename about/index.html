About Me

I’m a master student from Center for Future Media, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, under supervision of Prof. Song and Gao. My research interests lie in quantization for image retrieval, computer vision. I obtained my bachelor’s degree on Electrical Engineering and Auotmation in UESTC at year of 2017.

Github: https://github.com/xiaosu-zhu

Team: https://invokar.github.io/CFM-Lab.github.io/

  • Basic knowledge
